Nuedexta

Consumer Website

Nuedexta.com is the web component of a direct-to-consumer marketed pharmaceutical product. Ive led this project over the past 9 months to success in both consumer engagement and KPI results. In this project I was able to meet regulatory requirements and meet ADA/WCAG standards while elevating the site to a level that had not previously been accomplished. In addition to being responsible for the UX, I have continually worked with the off-site development team to strategize and optimize the technical aspects of the front-end and back-end of the site.

Humana

Consumer Website

As part of the complete redesign of Humana.com, I was tasked with gathering requirements from the client, analyzing and synthesizing research data, constructing a sitemap, and building a navigation model to test for the multi-billion dollar heath insurer. Throughout this process I worked with a cross-functional internal team and client team to produce the end result.

Symtuza

Consumer Website

Symtuza is a new HIV drug developed by Janssen, the pharmaceutical arm of Johnson & Johnson, that was approved and launched in 2018. While on this project I helped design the initial patient site, patient portals, an unbranded patient experience and a game for consumers. As part of this project I worked with the client to devlop ways to elevate key KPIs and customer actions to move upper-funnel prospects down the funnel. I collaborated with the development team as well to implement correct analytic tagging and improve web accessibility.

Charles Schwab

B2B Mobile App & Web Platform

I led a project to redesign and architect a B2B financial compliance solution that utilized the end product to drive assets to the parent company. As part of this project I was given two weeks, and using my own adaptation of the Google Ventures Design Sprint, I designed a conceptual mobile experience for completing compliance needs for both compliance administrators and end employees. This was presented to both the company and their advisory board for feedback and evaluation.

HealthyAmplified

Responsive Web Platform

Healthy Amplified is a injury reporting platform targeted towards little league and traveling sports teams. The platform is designed to track and manage injuries within a team and comply with certain laws that apply to head injuries with activities. The end product is a web and mobile solution that allows organizations, coaches, athletic trainers, athletes, and parents manage and track these injuries though the entire process. The project involved integrating medical databases and prescription databases.The project was invested in by Milliman in 2017.
